Veg Biryani Recipe


  You will need:

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice, long grain, soaked for 20 minutes in 2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoon Milk
  • 1 pinch Saffron (Kesar)
  • 1 ½ tablespoon Ghee
  • ½onion
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • ¼ tsp Plain Yoghurt
  • ¼ cup fried onions
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2 tablespoon Cilantro leaves
  • 3 Green Cardamom (Elaichi)
  • 4 Cloves (Laung)
  • 8 Black Peppercorns
  • 1 Potato
  • ½cup Green Peas
  • 1 cup Cauliflowers
  • ½ Red bell pepper
  • ½ cup Carrots
